# An example dataset from the Framingham study

An example dataset from the Framingham study

## Usage

``` r
data(framingham)
```

## Format

A data frame with nine variables:

- `randid`:

  id for each patient

- `period`:

  period of the measure study

- `sex`:

  sex of the patient

- `age`:

  age of the patient

- `totchol`:

  total cholesterol of the patient in mg/dL

- `hdlc`:

  hdl of the patient in mg/dL

- `ldlc`:

  ldl of the patient in mg/dL

- `sysbp`:

  systolic blood pressure of the patient in mmHg

- `diabp`:

  diastolic blood pressure of the patient in mmHg

- `cursmoke`:

  if the patient is currently smoking

- `diabetes`:

  if the patient is diagnosed with diabetes

- `anychd`:

  if the patient have suffered any CHD event during follow-up

- `timechd`:

  time of follow up of the patient

a dataset with 11627 rows and 13 variables
